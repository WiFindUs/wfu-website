FRIENDS

The Serval Project is a not-for-profit enterprise with the objective of connecting all people and bridging the digital divide through the creation of innovative technologies that have infrastructure-independence as their defining characteristic.

Flinders University is a leading public university in South Australia. The university has established a reputation as a leading research institution with a devotion to innovation.

The Flinders Event Safety Research (FUESR) is a not-for-profit Flinders University research group who aim to improve the safety of Outdoor Music Festivals through the use of real-time data monitoring technologies. FUESR have strong connections within the South Australian and national event industry, as well as links to the industry in Sweden and Serbia.

Air-Stream Wireless is a non-profit community group who use Wireless LAN in combination with software and other technologies to deploy a Wide Area Network (WAN) that supports community participation, local content and communications.
