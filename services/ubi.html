Ubi

An asset management system for* event organisers & industry service providers

Ubi is an Asset Management System. It provides unique communication solutions* to improve the health & safety of festivals and enhance their patrons' experience.

Eve

Eve, our low cost and rapidly deployable mesh network ensures that our clients no longer have to worry about congested data transfer at mass gathering events. It provides real-time interactivity that works independently from existing telecommunication infrastructure.

Eye

Within this local network, we deploy Eye: software built specifically to the needs of event organisers and industry service providers, utilising the most current technology to bring innovation to the industry.

Features

Real Time

Our solution provides real-time positioning information of on-ground security and first-aid assets, allowing for optimal allocation of assets throughout the event area.

2-way Communication

The system allows for two-way data communication between on-ground assets and the command centre, facilitating in the identification and response of incidents.
