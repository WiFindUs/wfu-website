Our Solutions

Proven Technologies - Revolutionary Applications

Real Time

We provide real-time position tracking solutions for personnel and assets. Our solution is accurate and flexible, catering for a variety of environments and applications.

2-way Communications

We facilitate and expand the communication capabilities between on-ground personnel and command centres through intuitive user interfaces designed for a variety of devices.

Mesh Network

Our flexible and rapidly deployable system has been designed to function independently from existing telecommunication infrastructure, ensuring reliable data transfer.

Core Features

Personnel Tracking

Real-time location information is the very core of what we do. From tracking the location of a large number of security officers at a music festival, to following the movements of a first-aid responder to an incident location, to tracking the progress of a paintball team chasing to capture a flag deep within enemy territory, our system will show you the way.

Maps

We have designed user interfaces to cater for a variety of applications and user needs. Whether you are in an event command centre managing a large team and striving for an efficient allocation of on-ground assets, or a medical officer following a navigation path on their mobile device through a crowded mosh pit and around a stage, or even a paintballer wearing a heads-up-display and rushing towards the next checkpoint, our purpose built 2D and 3D maps will help you get there.

Network Reliability and Flexibility

Our temporary, rapidly deployable, and independent network is what allows it all to happen. We use a mesh protocol that ensures complete flexibility to a variety of environments, no matter how large the area of coverage. To ensure complete reliability, we control and prioritise data traffic to efficiently utilise the available network capacity.
